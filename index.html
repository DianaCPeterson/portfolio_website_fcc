About Diana

Diana is a New York based writer and operations manager with expertise in startup operations. She is a native Californian and is interested in all things New York City.

Diana knows how to make things run. From operations to content, she has supported startups from seed funding to acquisition. Diana firmly believes that no project is too small, and no project is too big.

Capable of wearing many hats, Diana has managed day-to-day and sales operations, and supported C-suite executives as an executive assistant. Diana has introduced basic human resources processes while managing the office and facilities.

In her life outside of the office Diana focuses on her two lifetime passions: creative writing and photography. She is an avid short fiction writer who has worked on several television shows, including “The Good Wife.” As a photographer Diana focuses on street, nature and art photography. ​Each day she documents New York life in a photography project that has spanned over 1,600 days (and counting). Her passions enable her to bring creativity and insight into any project she contributes to.
